Brij World

Brij.world is a tech-logistics company that streamlines e-commerce and its consumers, offering easy and affordable shipping services from a US web store to over 30 states in Nigeria. We provide our customers with a US Virtual address to which they may ship their orders purchased from US online stores, to avoid the cost and hassles associated with traditional shipping to Nigeria.
